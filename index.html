A remastered version of Fate/stay night, fantasy action drama visual novel and Type-Moon's first-ever commercial release in 2004, has been released on Steam® for its 20th anniversary.

The story revolves around Shirou Emiya, a hardworking and honest teenager who unwillingly enters a to-the-death tournament called the Fifth Holy Grail War, where combatants fight with magecraft and Heroes from throughout history for a chance to have their wishes granted by the eponymous Holy Grail.
